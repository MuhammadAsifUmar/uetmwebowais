Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include Intermediate (FSc Pre-Engineering) with minimum 60% marks, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects, and meeting age requirements. Visit our admissions page for complete details.

How can I schedule a campus visit?

You can schedule a campus visit by contacting our admissions office at +92-937-123457 or emailing admissions@uetmardan.edu.pk. We offer guided tours on weekdays.

What programs are currently accepting applications?

We're currently accepting applications for Fall 2024 intake for all undergraduate and graduate programs. Check our admissions page for specific deadlines and requirements.

How can I contact a specific faculty member?

You can find faculty contact information on our department pages. For general inquiries, email the department directly or contact our main office.
